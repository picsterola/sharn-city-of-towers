Districts & Venues

Locations of Sharn

Sharn is a vertical city. From the gilded spires of Skyway to the soot-choked depths of the Cogs, each level tells a different story. Every district has its own character, its own dangers, and its own secrets.

Upper City

The Heights

Skyway

The wealthiest district, floating above the city. Home to the new Imperial Skydocks, Lady Halvaren's estate, and the Ascension Race finish line. Site of dissident protests scattered by the Imperial Guard.

Upper Central

Mithral Tower and Platinum Heights districts. Mystery climber sightings. Sannid's Restaurant (site of House Vyrune's imperial soiree). The seat of old money.

Upper Menthis Plateau

The cultural heart. Home to the Art Temple, the Grand Stage, the Domus Gallery, the Ezrekiel Gallery, and The Aundairian Easel restaurant. Where art and politics collide.

Middle City

The Spine

Middle Menthis

Far Hearth, Lady Ilyra Boromar's estate (burned). The Pitt House Theatre in Lower Menthis. Residential and commercial districts mixing nobility with merchants.

Northedge

Home to The Pickled Rose, where Vekel Vekelsson tells tales of adventure. A quieter residential ward away from the political heat.

Tavick's Landing

Rat infestations, the Gear-Breaker's bomb plot, and the Three Feasts of Aundair parade route. Also the Scavenger Hunt route. A working district under pressure.

Lower City

The Depths

Lower Dura

Gang headquarters (Mama Pain and Her Boyz, massacred). Dauntless Publishing operates from a restored press here. The Gilded Albatross hangar. The Hollow Hope orphanage. Dangerous but vital.

The Cogs

The industrial underbelly. Coppernock Foundry fire. Iron Foundry work stoppages. Felicity Amaryth's Whisperstone dig beneath the Cogs. Disease outbreaks. Where the city's real work happens.

Dura Penitentiary

Site of a jail riot that left a guard dead. The dark machinery of imperial justice.
