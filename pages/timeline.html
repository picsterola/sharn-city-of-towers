Chronology

Timeline of Major Events

A chronological record of the events that have shaped Sharn, as reported by The Sharn Inquisitive from November 2024 through February 2026.

November 2024

Pact Day Celebrations

Anarchists assault noble carriages. House Hanassa brokers peace between Corvoni cousins. The Del Coeur Emerald stolen from the Museum of Imperial Romantic History. Lord Jeramiah Cecilio killed in a duel.

February 2025

Gala Gone Wrong & Airship Bomb

Lady Ilyra Boromar's gala at Far Hearth ends in fire. PCA&ARI event aboard the Lightning Swift: bomb discovered, spine devils attack, Dauntless Agency saves the day. Pitt House Theatre closes after Duchess B--'s duel kills 10.

March 2025

Preparations & Plague

Emperor's Silver Jubilee preparations begin. Morgrave expedition disappears into the Deep. New sickness in Lower Cogs with high mortality. Greensinger Rebellion quashed. Lady Nora ir'Roole flees after attempted mariticide.

June 2025

Art Theft Wave & Underground Discovery

Horizon Wellness Institute closes. Mama Pain and Her Boyz massacred. Vigil departs for Aundair. Art theft at Ezrekiel Gallery. Felicity Amaryth discovers the Whisperstone dig. Tears of the Angels stolen from Mithral Tower.

July 2025

Morgrave Murders & Gloom Reports

Lysander Vyrune joins Morgrave Board of Provosts. Professors Kaelem and Lorianna found dead. Soldiers return from Eldeen Reaches. Reports of Gloom-free lands beyond the Shard Mountains. Fire at Coppernock Foundry.

September 2025

The Silver Jubilee & Ministry Shake-Up

Month-long imperial celebration begins. Lord Akmenos "retires" as Minister of Information; Cassian Moreau installed. Imperial Skydocks open in Skyway. Mystery climber spotted in Upper Central. Nora Murlock seen with Lord Malakai ir'Roole.

October 2025

Justice & Jubilee

Councilor Jiran ir'Tain found guilty of three murders, executed. All ir'Tain assets seized. Silver Jubilee concludes with a 25,000-person concert. Gear-Breaker bomber arrested. Lady Katherine ir'Vown endows Morgrave library wing.

November 2025

Institutional Decay Deepens

Student protests at Morgrave. Sharn Watch officer exodus. Court backlog from justiciar retirements. Iron Foundry work stoppage. The Whispering Court debuts at the Art Temple. Lady Claudia Aemilius and Lord Flavius Mericure spotted together.

February 2026

The Ascension Race & Rising Threats

The Ascension Race returns. Lady Halvaren vanishes mid-toast. Golden glow in Lower Tavick's Landing sewers. Orphans witness "dragon" smuggled through Lower Dura. Gargoyle falls through Mithral Tower ballroom. The Emerald Claw recruits openly. Cecillio-"Lord & Lady S" wedding at The Gilded Lotus.

February 2026 — Special Edition

The Gilded Albatross Wins

Lianna ir'Clarn pilots The Gilded Albatross to victory with the Dauntless Agency aboard. The Bloodwind destroyed. The Paradigm and Marrow-Wake spiral into the Dagger River. Syndicate bomb plot at the finish line foiled. Lianna pays her father's debts to the Boromar Clan.
